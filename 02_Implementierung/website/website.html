International Students

Studying in Berlin offers a unique opportunity to immerse yourself in one of the most culturally vibrant cities in Europe.

There are numerous resources available to help you settle in, including language courses and student support services.

Berlin's affordable cost of living, compared to other European cities, makes it an attractive destination for students. With reasonable rent prices, an extensive public transportation system, and discounts for students at various venues, Berlin offers a cost-effective yet dynamic environment.

Experiencing Berlin’s vibrant student life – where dynamic campuses, diverse cultures, and a thriving urban vibe create the perfect backdrop for your academic and personal growth.
